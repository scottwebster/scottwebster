--- layout: page title: Services permalink: /services/ weight: 4 hero_background: /img/hero/services.jpg --- Scott is available to provide a wide-range of individual and group outdoor activities, from hill walking through to winter mountaineering.

Rock climbing

Scott is a fully qualified rock-climbing instructor and is available to run courses in the UK from introductory taster sessions through to learning to lead climb and advanced self-rescue techniques.

  • £160 + £40 per additional person
  • Max group size: 2

Scrambling

Scott is fully qualified to both instruct and guide people in scrambling techniques throughout the UK. Scott has spent several seasons working on the Cullin Ridge, Isle of Skye – home to some of the best scrambling in the UK.

  • £160 + £40 per additional person
  • Max group size: 2

Hill walking

Scott is available to lead groups hill walking in both summer and winter conditions. This can include overnight trips wild camping or bivvying.

  • £120 + £40 per additional person
  • Max group size: 6

Navigation

If you want to brush up on your navigational skills then Scott can run sessions in the UK to help. Courses can be pitched to any level, from complete beginner to advanced micro-nav techniques for use in poor weather and at night.

  • £120 + £40 for an additional person
  • Max group size: 5

Winter skills

If you are interested in making the progression from summer hill walking to winter then Scott can provide instruction in the required skills and techniques. This includes the use of crampons and ice axes as well as training on safe route choice and avalanche awareness/avoidance.

  • £150 + £40 per additional person
  • Max group size: 4

Winter climbing

Scott is not currently qualified to teach winter climbing in the UK but aims to complete his MIC (the UK qualification that certifies winter climbing instruction) in Winter 2015/16.

Overseas expeditions

If you are looking for a qualified UK leader for an overseas expedition, Scott has extensive worldwide travelling experience and can lead expeditions to a variety of locations.

Please get in touch to discuss your individual requirements.
